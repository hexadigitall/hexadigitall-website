# Email Nurture Sequences for Social Campaign

## Setup & Automation

### Email Tool Setup (Recommended: Mailchimp or HubSpot free tier)
1. Create a landing page form (email + first name minimum)
2. Set form to trigger email sequence based on service/city
3. Use UTM tags to auto-segment (e.g., ?utm_source=facebook&utm_service=web)
4. Schedule sends: Day 0 (immediate), Day 1, Day 3, Day 5, Day 7

### Segmentation by Service
- Web: sends "Web Dev Sequence"
- Marketing: sends "Digital Marketing Sequence"
- Ecommerce: sends "E-Commerce Sequence"
- Business Plans: sends "Business Plans Sequence"
- Portfolio: sends "Portfolio Sequence"
- Courses: sends "Courses Sequence"

### Send Times (Nigeria Time)
- Day 0: Immediate (5 min post-optin)
- Day 1: 9 AM (morning energy high)

## Email Copy Tone & Style

**Always:**
- Write like a human (not corporate)
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Start with benefit (hook first sentence)
- Use specific numbers (â‚¦500k, 14 days, 50 leads)
- End with clear CTA (reply, click button, or book call)

**Never:**
- ALL CAPS (unless emphasis on one word)
- 10+ paragraphs (wall of text = 0% opens)
- Vague CTAs ("Click here" vs. "Book free consultation")
- Multiple CTAs per email (one ask per email)
