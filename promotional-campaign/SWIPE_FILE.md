## PLATFORM RULES & TIPS

### Facebook
- **Length:** 200â€“500 words ideal (algorithm favors longer engagement)
- **CTA:** Always ask a question or call for comment (algorithm boosts posts with comments)
- **Emojis:** 3â€“5 per post (breaks up text visually)
- **Pin tactic:** Pin top comment as reply to drive more engagement
- **Best times:** 8 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM Nigeria time
- **Tone:** Conversational, warm, problem-solver

### Instagram
- **Story length:** 1-2 sentences max (stops scrolls, not reads)
- **Feed caption:** 150â€“250 words (not too long, easy to scan)
- **Hashtags:** 5â€“10 per post (#HexadigitallNG + city + industry tags)
- **Emojis:** 10+ per post (Instagram algorithm loves emoji engagement)
- **Stickers:** Poll, Question, Quiz on Stories (drives interaction)
- **Bio link:** Always point to city-specific landing page
- **Best times:** 1 PM, 6 PM Nigeria time
- **Tone:** Visual-first, aspirational, youth-leaning

### Twitter/X
- **Thread length:** 5â€“7 tweets (enough to be useful, not overwhelming)
- **Retweet boost:** Retweet own post +2 hours for algorithm reach
- **Quote tweets:** Reply to industry debates/challenges (hijack trending)
- **Length:** 140â€“280 chars (leave room for engagement)
- **Emojis:** 2â€“3 per tweet (enough to stand out, not spammy)
- **Best times:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 7 PM Nigeria time

### WhatsApp Business

- **Follow-up:** Send personal voice note within 2 hours (builds trust)
