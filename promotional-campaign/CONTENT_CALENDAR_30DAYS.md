## Platform Guidelines

### Facebook (Best for community + conversions)
- Post 8 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM Nigeria time
- Always ask a question or call for comment
- Pin top engaging comment as reply
- Use city name in first line for local relevance
- Include CTA button (Learn More / Book Now)

### Instagram (Best for reach + engagement)
- Post 1 PM + 6 PM Nigeria time
- Use Stories for daily teasers (8 AM, 3 PM)
- Stories: interactive stickers (poll, question, quiz)
- Captions: 3-5 lines, emoji, hashtags (5-10 per post)
- Bio link: Always point to city-specific landing page
- Cross-promotion: Stories ‚Üí Feed ‚Üí DM

### Twitter/X (Best for speed + virality)
- Post 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 7 PM Nigeria time
- Retweet own posts +2 hours for algorithm boost
- Threads (on Mondays) about service deep-dives
