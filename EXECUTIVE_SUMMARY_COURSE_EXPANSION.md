## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Teacher Shortage
**Mitigation**: 
- Pre-screen contractors during Phase A
- Offer competitive rates (‚Ç¶100K-300K/month per teacher)
- Build 2-3 teacher backlog before launch

### Risk 2: Quality Degradation
**Mitigation**:
- Implement teacher onboarding program
- Monthly 1:1 check-ins with all instructors
- Student feedback review process
- Standardized course materials/templates

### Risk 3: Market Saturation
**Mitigation**:
- Differentiate on PPP pricing model
- Focus on underserved markets (Africa, emerging markets)
- Build strong instructor brands
- Create ecosystem (certifications, job placements)

### Risk 4: Paystack Payment Issues
**Mitigation**:
- Implement dev-only mock for testing
- Add fallback payment method (Stripe, local transfers)
- Improve error handling & customer support
