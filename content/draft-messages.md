## ğŸ¯ Usage Guidelines

### Best Practices

**Timing:**
- **WhatsApp/Instagram**: Best during business hours (10 AM - 6 PM)
- **Facebook**: Can send anytime; they'll see it when available
- **Email**: Send Tuesday-Thursday mornings for best open rates

**Tone:**
- **WhatsApp**: Friendly but professional
- **Instagram**: Casual, use emojis, keep it brief
- **Facebook**: Professional with personality
- **Email**: Formal-friendly, detailed

**Follow-Up:**
- Wait 3-4 days before first follow-up
- Send gentle reminder: "Just checking if you had a chance to review..."
- After 2 follow-ups with no response, wait 2 weeks then send final message

### Personalization Checklist

Before sending any message, customize:
- [ ] Replace `[Your Name]` with actual name
- [ ] Verify phone number is correct
- [ ] Check email address is current
- [ ] Mention specific product/post you saw (shows genuine interest)
- [ ] Adjust tone based on their brand personality
- [ ] Update any time-sensitive offers (December deadline, etc.)

### Response Scenarios

**If they're interested:**
- Send proposal document immediately
- Offer to schedule call within 24-48 hours
- Be prepared with calendar link

**If they have questions:**
- Answer promptly (within 2-4 hours if possible)
- Offer call to clarify
- Send relevant case studies or examples

**If they say "maybe later":**
- Thank them for considering
- Ask when would be a good time to follow up
- Add to CRM for future contact
- Send periodic value content (tips, industry news)

**If they say "too expensive":**
- Emphasize value and ROI
- Offer payment plan
- Suggest starting with Basic package
- Share success stories/testimonials

**If no response:**
- Follow up once after 3 days
- Follow up again after 7 days
- Final touch after 2 weeks
- Don't be pushy; be helpful
