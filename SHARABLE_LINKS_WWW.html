Stories/Statuses Pro Tips

1. Frequency: Post 2-3 stories daily per platform for maximum reach. Morning (9 AM), Midday (12 PM), Evening (6 PM).
2. Engagement Hooks: Use polls, questions, countdowns, timers, quizzes, "This or That"‚Äîask audience to interact!
3. Urgency Tactics: "Last 24 hours", "Spots disappearing", "Midnight deadline", "Only today"‚Äîcreate FOMO.
4. Visuals Matter: Behind-the-scenes (BTS) content performs 5x better. Instructor videos, classroom clips, student reactions.
5. Hashtag Smart: Add 3-5 hashtags per story caption. Instagram stories show in story search results‚Äîuse trending tags!
6. Link Strategy: Instagram/Facebook "Swipe Up" links drive traffic 10x more than feed posts. Use exclusively for CTAs.
7. Story Series: Create narrative arcs across 3-5 stories. Build curiosity ‚Üí reveal answer ‚Üí CTA. Example: "Guess salary" ‚Üí "Reveal" ‚Üí "Enroll".
8. Sticker Power: Poll, Question, Chat, Quiz, Countdown, Timer stickers = 3x engagement vs plain text.
9. Repost User Content: Students posting success? Repost to your story (tag them). Social proof + community love.
10. Consistency Wins: Same posting times = audience expects you. Algorithm rewards consistency. Auto-post tools help here!
