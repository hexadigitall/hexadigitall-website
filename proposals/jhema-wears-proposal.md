### Usage Instructions for Sharable Links

1. **Testing Your Links** (before launch):
   - Use [Facebook Sharing Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/)
   - Enter your product URL
   - Click "Scrape Again" to refresh preview
   - Verify image and text display correctly

2. **Sharing on Social Media**:
   - Copy product URL from your website
   - Paste into Facebook/WhatsApp/Instagram bio
   - Preview will automatically generate
   - Customers can click through to purchase

3. **Best Practices**:
   - Always use full product URLs (not shortened links initially)
   - Share new products within 24 hours of adding to site
   - Test links in Facebook Debugger before major campaigns
   - Update product images for seasonal campaigns

### Instagram Bio Link Strategy
Since Instagram doesn't show link previews in bio, we recommend:
- Create a beautiful "Shop Now" landing page
