Key Social Media Terms

Term Definition Example
USP Unique Selling Proposition - what sets your brand apart. Eco-friendly fabrics or bold prints
Engagement Rate Percentage of followers who interact with your content. Likes, comments, shares, and saves
Conversion When a follower takes a desired action. Clicking "Shop Now" and making a purchase
Reach Number of unique people who saw your post. 10,000 different users viewed your video
Impressions Total times your content was displayed. One person saw your ad 3 times = 3 impressions
Content Calendar Schedule of planned posts. Planning Instagram posts every Friday
Community Management Engaging with followers to build relationships. Replying to comments and DMs

Brand Personality Terms

Term Definition Example
Edgy Bold, daring, and unconventional. Neon colors and unexpected cuts
Minimalist Clean, simple, and uncluttered. Neutral-toned capsule wardrobe
Playful Fun, lighthearted, and approachable. Bright colors with quirky captions
Elegant Sophisticated, refined, and timeless. Couture gown with polished visuals
