Appendix: Glossary of key terms
Term Definition Example / Context
USP (Unique Selling Proposition) The distinct value or quality that sets your brand apart. A fashion house known for eco-friendly fabrics or bold prints.
KPI (Key Performance Indicator) A measurable value showing progress toward goals. Tracking how many sales come from Instagram ads.
Engagement Rate The percentage of followers who interact with your content. Likes, comments, shares, and saves on a new collection post.
Conversion When a follower takes a desired action. Clicking “Shop Now” and buying a dress.
Reach The number of unique people who saw your post. 10,000 different users viewed your runway teaser video.
Impressions The total times your content was displayed. One person saw your ad three times — that’s three impressions.
Content Calendar A schedule of planned posts. Planning Instagram reels for product launches every Friday.
Community Management Engaging with followers to build relationships. Replying to DMs about sizing or thanking fans for comments.
Paid Ads Sponsored posts designed to reach more people. Boosting a new collection launch to target fashion lovers in Lagos.
Influencer Collaboration Partnering with trusted voices to promote your brand. A stylist with 50k followers showcasing your latest handbag.
