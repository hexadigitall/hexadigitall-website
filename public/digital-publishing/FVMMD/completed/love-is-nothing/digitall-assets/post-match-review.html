The Post-Match Review Tool

Execute this rapid review loop together after a heated disagreement has fully cooled down. Do not walk away or pretend it didn't happen. Spend five minutes to find the root cause, change the operational routine, and mix real mortar.

Track 1 // Debris Management
1.1 Let's trace the actual anatomy of the friction. What specific event or remark triggered the immediate spike in tension today?
1.2 Look beneath the surface event. What percentage of this disagreement was truly about today's issue, and what percentage was driven by an old, hidden crack we papered over recently?

Track 2 // Mortar Application
2.1 Verbal apologies are just temporary patches. What is the explicit, daily operational adjustment we are both committing to right now to keep this specific gap sealed?
2.2 Validation Check: Does our teammate feel fully heard and supported as we conclude this review loop, or is there a lingering line we need to dig out?
