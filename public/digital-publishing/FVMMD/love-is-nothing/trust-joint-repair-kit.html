The Trust Joint Repair Kit

When trust is significantly fractured, simple spoken apologies act as poor structural patches. This remediation framework shifts focus entirely from words to verifiable behavioral data over a structured 30-day implementation loop.

Track 1 // Excavation & Concrete Commitments
1.1 The Excavation Phase: What core operational action or breach of transparency caused this structural breakdown? Dig away the surface symptoms and identify the foundational failure mode explicitly.
1.2 Concrete Mortar Rules: What are the three non-negotiable, verifiable boundaries or transparency habits required over the next 30 days to provide structural shoring for the relationship?

Track 2 // 30-Day Joint Inspection
2.1 Consistency Audit: Review the 30-day performance data loop. Did both partners follow through on the concrete mortar rules established in Track 1? Note any moments where the support structures wobbled.
