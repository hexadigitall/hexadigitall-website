Welcome to Ansible Automation & IaC Fundamentals! 🎓

This entry-level 20-week professional engineering program is meticulously architected to take you from a true configuration novice to a capable Infrastructure Automation Engineer. We start directly from absolute scratch—establishing safe terminal environments, compiling atomic playbooks, configuring inventory networks, and parsing structured YAML abstraction schemas safely before moving on to multi-tier deployment suites.

This program acts as the mandatory prerequisite stepping stone before proceeding to our specialized Advanced Ansible Automation & IaC masterclass.

Your engineering development is our mission. You will progress across 4 strategic technical phases, completing at least two dedicated laboratory exercises every single week to build reproducible, production-grade automation skills.

Prerequisites & Entry Criteria

Terminal Familiarity

Basic comfort operating inside a Linux terminal shell environment (navigating pathways, reading files, executing scripts).

Text Abstractions

Elementary exposure to common data structuring formats or descriptive text markup layouts (JSON, XML, or basic properties layouts).

System Access Baselines

Awareness of remote machine connectivity parameters, including general key-pair verification mechanics and target host ports.

Professional Target

A firm commitment to discard manual server-by-server clicking habits in favor of deterministic, declarative configuration tracking models.
