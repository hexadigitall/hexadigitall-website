Consulting packages

Discovery & Assessment

  • Initial consultation call (1-2 hours)
  • Business assessment and analysis
  • Competitive landscape review
  • Opportunity and risk identification
  • Executive summary with 5-7 recommendations
  • Resources and tools guide
  • Follow-up email consultation

Advisory Package

  • Everything in Discovery & Assessment
  • 2 months of ongoing advisory (bi-weekly calls)
  • Implementation roadmap and action plan
  • Team workshop or team coaching session
  • Priority email/message support
  • Progress tracking and KPI monitoring
  • Mid-point review and strategy adjustment

Full Implementation

  • Everything in Advisory
  • 6 months of intensive partnership
  • Weekly strategic calls
  • Hands-on implementation support
  • Multiple team workshops (3-5 sessions)
  • Custom tools and process design
  • Change management support
  • Dedicated Slack/communication channel
  • Final results report and transition plan

What we need from you

  • Overview of your business, products/services, and market position.
  • Current challenges, pain points, or areas for improvement.
  • Business goals and success metrics for the next 12 months.
  • Team structure and key stakeholder information.
  • Relevant historical data, metrics, or financials (if applicable).
  • Your availability for calls and implementation.
