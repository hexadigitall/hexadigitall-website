## Facebook Page Message Tips

### Before Sending

1. **Like and engage with their recent posts** (2-3 posts)
2. **Wait 1-2 days** before sending a DM
3. **Check if they have a website already**
4. **Note their posting schedule** and engagement level
5. **Screenshot their best products** for reference

### Sending Strategy

1. **React to their content first** (show genuine interest)
2. **Comment thoughtfully on 1-2 posts**
3. **Then send a DM** referencing what you liked
4. **Be patient** - Facebook DMs can be slow
5. **Follow up strategically** (3 days, 1 week, then stop)

### What to Avoid

âŒ Don't send immediate generic DMs
âŒ Don't spam with multiple messages at once
âŒ Don't only talk about yourself
âŒ Don't be pushy or aggressive
âŒ Don't criticize their current setup
âŒ Don't send during odd hours (late night/early morning)
