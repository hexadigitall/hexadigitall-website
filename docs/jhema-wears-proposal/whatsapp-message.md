## Tips for Sending

### Best Practices

1. **Timing:**
   - Best: 10 AM - 12 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM (Nigerian time)
   - Avoid: Very early morning, late night
   - Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

2. **Personalization:**
   - Use their brand name
   - Reference specific products you liked
   - Show you've researched their business

3. **Tone:**
   - Professional but friendly
   - Enthusiastic but not pushy
   - Helpful, not salesy

4. **Length:**
   - Keep it concise
   - Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per paragraph)
   - Break up text with line breaks

5. **Call-to-Action:**
   - Make it clear and easy
   - Offer options (call or message)
   - No pressure

### What to Avoid

âŒ Don't send too many messages at once
âŒ Don't be pushy or aggressive
âŒ Don't use too much technical jargon
